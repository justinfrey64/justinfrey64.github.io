About me

Hello! My name is Justin Frey and I'm a Fullstack Developer and Web Designer. I'm currently employed by a large corporation as a Programmer but I'm always looking for new and exciting oppurtunities.

I have a passion for creating websites that are both engaging and pleasing to the eye. I enjoy writing clean and responsive front-ends to pair with intuitive and functional back-ends.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy going snowboarding, adventuring with friends in Dungeons and Dragons, reading Game of Thrones, and playing video games.

Services

This is what I do

Front-end Development

Designing front-ends is my favorite part of coding. Whether it be a website for the world to see or the front-end to an enterprise application, I like being able to see what all my hard work made. The tools I use to do this include HTML, CSS, JavaScript and frameworks like jQuery and Bootstrap. I'm also currently in the process of learning Angular, React, and Sass.

Back-end Development

Can't have a polished front-end without a functional back-end. Most of the time, my application/logic layer coding is done with Java but I also have experience with Python, Ruby, and C++. As for my data access layer, I mainly use SQL and have learned PHP as well. I have worked with both DB2 and Microsoft SQL databases.

Responsive Design

I feel like responsive design is the best way to get people to take a look at a website. Responsive design ensures that no matter what device someone is using, everything looks good and well organized. The tools I have used to accomplish this include Bootstrap, Materialize, and Content Management Systems (CMS) like WordPress and Drupal.
